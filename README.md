# ॐ Shoonya: The Divine Algorithm

Shoonya is a sophisticated, clutter-free mobile application designed to guide users through a structured 60-day transformation. Bridging ancient spiritual practices with ultra-modern technology, the app provides a highly intentional environment for tracking yoga, meditation, nutrition, and shifting states of consciousness.

Developed by the Guild Of Divine Souls (GODS).

## ✨ Key Features

*   **The 60-Day Roadmap:** A beautifully structured journey divided into 7 distinct phases, from *Environmental Cleansing* to *Integration & Independence*.
*   **Intelligent Daily Practice:** A seamless "Today" dashboard featuring a 1–10 scoring system for daily activities, complete with an immersive "Focus Active" mode to silence internal distractions during practice.
*   **Map of Consciousness Integration:** A dynamic tracker based on Dr. David R. Hawkins' emotional scale, allowing users to log their current energetic frequency (from 20 to 1000) and track their elevation.
*   **On-Demand AI Mentorship:** An integrated AI guide with seamless switching between premium models (Gemini 2.5 Flash/Pro, GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet/Haiku) to answer questions regarding practice and inner journeying.
*   **Comprehensive Wellness Vault:** Deep tracking for hydration, weight, energy, and sleep visualized through elegant curved graphs, plus dedicated spaces for nutrition logging and medical records.
*   **Privacy & Teacher Visibility:** Granular controls allowing users to dictate exactly what data (journals, medical reports) is visible to their human guides.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

*   **Framework:** React Native / Expo (Expo Router)
*   **State Management:** Zustand
*   **Data Visualization:** React Native Graph
*   **Styling:** Custom theming engine (Tailwind/Native styling)
*   **Backend / Database:** Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage)
*   **Icons & UI:** Lucide React Native
